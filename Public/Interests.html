A brief description of my Interests

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence fascinates me because it sits at the intersection of logic and learning—building systems that improve through data rather than rigid rules. I’m especially drawn to how models reason, adapt, and fail in interesting ways.

Data Science

Data Science appeals to my need to extract signal from noise. I enjoy working with raw data, cleaning it, analyzing patterns, and turning messy numbers into decisions that actually matter.

Linux Systems

Linux systems pull me in because they reward understanding over convenience. Working close to the operating system has sharpened my grasp of how software truly runs, not just how it’s launched.

Automation

Automation is my quiet obsession. If a task repeats, it deserves a script. I enjoy designing workflows that remove friction, reduce human error, and scale effort efficiently.

Language Learning (Japanese)

Language learning—currently Japanese—keeps my brain flexible. It trains pattern recognition, patience, and cultural awareness, all of which translate surprisingly well into programming and problem-solving.

Building Productivity Systems (Notion)

Building productivity systems in Notion is where structure meets experimentation. I design systems to track habits, knowledge, and goals, then refine them until they feel effortless rather than enforced.
